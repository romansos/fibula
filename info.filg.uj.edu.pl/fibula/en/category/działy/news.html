Information and dissemination

  • News

Within the project, from 2008 to march 2011 35 meetings-seminars were organized in Małopolska region. During the seminars, prepared for the dissemination and information purposes, the members of our team presented the overall history of the collection and some specific cases of interesting manuscripts providing a creative stimulus to participants. Sometimes inspiring articles like this one (in Polish) in the website of the Library of the Economic University of Cracow.
